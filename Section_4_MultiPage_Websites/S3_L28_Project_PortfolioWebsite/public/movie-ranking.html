Michael's Top Movies

These are my 5 most favourite movies.

1: Real Steel

Absolute banger, giant robots boxing and an underdog who wins the fans heart and takes on the champ.
Badass and heartfelt.

2: Kung Fu Panda

A fat clumsy panda working at his adopted father's noodle shop gets chosen to be the dragon warrior.
But will he be ready to face the impending doom that broke free to claim his own title?

3: Premium Rush

A movie about a delivery cyclist who gets caught up in a black market dealing when he gets tasked with delivering a package above his paygrade.
Will he be able to get the package delivered to it's owner?

4: Treasure Planet

The original childhood dream.
What's there to not love about this?

5: Iron Man

A true classic. Tony Stark creates a powersuit that turns him into the modern hero.
Enough to make men weak at the knees.
